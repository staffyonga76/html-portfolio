About Me

I am someone who goes after what I want, even when it seems impossible to achieve. Throughout my journey, I have explored different paths and given my all to each one. I started with drama and dedicated myself fully to it. When it did not work out, I accepted that it was not my true calling because I had already given it my heart and soul.

I then moved into content creation. I spent time studying the craft through YouTube and remained highly consistent. At first, I achieved good results with strong views and engagement, but eventually things stopped growing. Even though I worked hard and stayed committed, I realized it was not the path meant for me.

After that, I began working as a Security Officer at MTN Group through Bidvest Protea Coin. During this time, I reflected deeply on my future and recognized my potential in IT. I had previously completed a Diploma in IT in college, but I had never specialized in a specific field. That led me to ask myself an important question: What do I truly enjoy the most? The answer was development.

I decided to invest in myself by purchasing a web development course by Angela Yu on Udemy and that decision changed everything for me. Web development feels natural to me — it feels like home. I am excited to continue growing my skills, building meaningful projects, and sharing my work with others. I look forward to opportunities where I can collaborate, contribute, and create value through development.
